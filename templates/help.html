‚úÖ Data Validation

What Gets Validated

  • Required Fields: All fields must be filled out
  • Email Format: Must be a valid email address format
  • Phone Number: Must contain 10-15 digits (formatting is flexible)
  • Text Length: Names and other text fields are validated for appropriate length

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

  • Email not accepted: Make sure it includes @ symbol and a valid domain
  • Phone not accepted: Ensure it has at least 10 digits (spaces, dashes, and parentheses are okay)
  • Form won't submit: Check that all required fields are filled out
  • Can't delete record: Make sure you confirm the deletion in the popup dialog
  • CSV won't download: Check your browser's download settings and popup blockers
üí° Pro Tip: You can format phone numbers in various ways like (123) 456-7890, 123-456-7890, or 1234567890 - they're all accepted!
